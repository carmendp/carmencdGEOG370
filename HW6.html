The map below is a chlopleth map of the state of Maryland'd counties. The counties a re color coded based on the amount of insured indivudals in each county. The amount of insured individuals in each county varies from approximately 19,000 to 1,000,000. The lowest amount of insured individuals are in the red counties, and the highest amount of insured individuals are represented by blue. The palette of the map is a gradient form warmest colorse being a lower amount of people to cooler colors being higher amount of insured people.
